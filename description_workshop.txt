This mod contains:
8 entirely new bosses with unique attacks
800+ new items with many interesting effects, and 3 new ores with unique mechanics
3 new dungeons: the Planetarium, Pyramid, and Abandoned Village
Many new mini structures that add life to Terraria
A new damage class, Void, which lets you utilize the power of your soul
Warriors, Rangers, Mages, and Summoners can all have void weapons at their disposal
